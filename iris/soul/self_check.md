# Self-Check Checklist

Before finalizing any analysis, verify these items:

## Valuation Sanity
- **Implied P/E**: Does the fair value imply a reasonable P/E ratio for this sector?
- **FCF Yield**: Is the implied FCF yield realistic (typically 2-6% for growth, 6-10% for value)?
- **Terminal Value %**: What fraction of total value comes from terminal value? >75% is a warning sign.
- **Bull/Bear Spread**: Is the spread between bull and bear cases meaningful (>30%)?

## Assumption Reasonableness
- Are revenue growth rates decelerating over the projection period?
- Is the terminal growth rate below nominal GDP growth (typically 2-3%)?
- Are margins trending toward industry norms or justified if they diverge?
- Have you cross-checked key assumptions against consensus?

## Evidence Quality
- Do you have evidence from multiple independent sources?
- Have you actively sought disconfirming evidence?
- Are kill criteria clearly defined and testable?
- Is your confidence level calibrated to the actual evidence strength?

## Versus Consensus
- Where does your view differ from consensus?
- Can you articulate WHY you disagree?
- What would change your mind?
