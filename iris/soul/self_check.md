# Self-Check

Before finalizing, sanity-check your work:
- Does the implied P/E make sense for this sector?
- Is terminal value >75% of total value? If so, your near-term assumptions may be too conservative.
- Did you actively seek disconfirming evidence, or only confirmatory data?
- Where does your view differ from consensus, and can you articulate why?
- What would change your mind?
